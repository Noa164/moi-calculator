MOI Calculator (Antibiotic Selection Assay)

Implements the MOI estimate: MOI = (Antibiotic+ survivors) / (Antibiotic− survivors). Enter duplicate counts for each condition and the page will average replicates, compute MOI, and optionally show Poisson-based infection fractions.

2) Puromycin working solution helper

Compute how much stock to add to reach your desired working concentration in each well. Defaults below reproduce the example for K562 (0.4 µg/mL from 100 µg/mL into 1000 µL → 4 µL).

Formula: Vstock = (Cwork / Cstock) × Vwell

Protocol sketch

  • Count viable cells.
  • Split each condition into duplicate wells: one receives puromycin, one remains as antibiotic-free control.
  • Add puromycin at the appropriate concentration for your cell line (e.g., K562: 0.4 µg/mL). Example calc shown above.
  • Incubate at 37 °C and monitor viability daily; record surviving counts.
  • Compute MOI = Antibiotic+ / Antibiotic− (replicate means).
